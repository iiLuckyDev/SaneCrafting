# SaneCrafting

SaneCrafting lets you craft many Slimefun Enhanced Crafting Table recipes in a normal Minecraft Crafting Table.

## How It Works

Once you have researched a Slimefun recipe that normally uses the Enhanced Crafting Table, you can craft it in a regular 3x3 crafting grid.

You do not need to do anything special. Use a Crafting Table like you would for a vanilla recipe.

## Recipe Book

Unlocked Enhanced Crafting Table recipes are also added to your vanilla Recipe Book.

This means you can:

- Find unlocked Slimefun crafting recipes in the Recipe Book.
- Click a recipe to autofill the Crafting Table grid.
- Craft the item from a normal Crafting Table if you have the correct ingredients.

Recipes only appear after you have researched them in Slimefun.

## If A Recipe Looks Craftable But Does Not Work

Sometimes the Recipe Book may highlight a Slimefun recipe as craftable even when you do not actually have the right ingredients.

This happens because Minecraft's Recipe Book does not fully understand Slimefun's custom items. It may see a similar-looking material and think the recipe can be made.

If the recipe does not craft:

- Check that you are using the correct Slimefun items, not just vanilla items that look similar.
- Check that you have researched the recipe.
- Check the recipe in your Slimefun Guide if you are unsure.

This is normal behavior and does not mean the recipe is broken.

## What Still Works The Same

- You still need to unlock recipes through Slimefun research.
- You still need the correct Slimefun ingredients.
- You can still use the Enhanced Crafting Table if you prefer.
- Unresearched recipes will not be available in the Recipe Book.
